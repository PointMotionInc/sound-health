Immersive Therapies for Aging Adults Are a Reality We’re Creating.<\/span><\/h2>\n

Transforming the future of healthcare for aging adults with neurological conditions is no longer a distant dream \u2013 it’s a reality we’re creating through the power of immersive and engaging therapeutic experiences. By combining cutting-edge research in physical and brain health with the latest technologies, music, movement, and gamification, we’re delivering a new standard of care for those at risk or diagnosed with conditions like Parkinson’s disease and dementia.
Join us in improving populations’ health and bringing music where people need it most.<\/span><\/p>\n

Our Story<\/h2>\n

It all started with a family accident. Kevin, our founder, watched as his cousin struggled to stay motivated during his exercise regimen for a speedy recovery. Kevin, who studied jazz composition and film scoring at Berklee College of Music, decided to use music to make the exercises more enjoyable for his cousin, but he soon realized that this method was not scalable. So, he set out to build a platform that could gamify therapies using the power of music and movement.<\/p>\n

Along the way, Imen joined Kevin as a co-founder. Imen, who holds a Master of Science in International Marketing, a Design Thinking Executive Education from Stanford Graduate School of Business, and a passion for emerging technologies, brought valuable business acumen to the team. Together, they assembled a strong network of partners, a board of directors, and advisors to tackle this problem on a larger scale.<\/span><\/p>\n

Despite multiple failures at building and positioning the platform, the team persevered and eventually welcomed Aman aboard. Aman, a product guru who formerly worked for notable companies, brought his expertise to help build the technology and focus on one of the most under-served audiences: seniors.<\/span><\/p>\n

Today, our team has successfully built a platform that allows seniors to enjoy their therapies in a gamified way from the comfort of their homes. Our platform also collects valuable clinical data to help support decisions when managing chronic conditions. We are proud to offer a solution that not only revolutionizes the healthcare industry but also improves the quality of life for seniors everywhere.<\/span><\/p>\n
